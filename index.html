♨️ Pool & Hot Tub Instructions

The pool and spa are controlled by a Pentair EasyTouch panel located at the pool equipment area. Follow the steps below.

▶️
Step 1 — Turn On
Press the SPA button on the control panel
The LED lights up and the filter pump starts. The valves rotate into position — the pump will briefly pause during this (normal). The heater activates automatically once water is circulating.
🌡️
Step 2 — Set Temperature
Use the + / − buttons to adjust the set temp
Recommended range: 100–104°F. “Heater” appears on the display while actively heating. The heater shuts off automatically once the set temperature is reached.
⏹️
Step 3 — Turn Off
Press the SPA button again to turn off
The LED turns off and the system shuts down. If the heater was running, the pump may continue circulating for up to 10 minutes to cool the heater — this is normal and expected.
⚠️
Good to Know
The spa automatically shuts off after 24 hours
Always replace the cover when finished to retain heat. If the water appears cloudy or has a strong chemical odor, please do not use it and contact T3D Properties right away.
